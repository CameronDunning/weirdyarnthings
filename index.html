Welcome To Weird Yarn Things

Our Story

We started out as a one-lady show with a passion for yarn. But we didn't know what to knit! So we came up with a few things. First a disc cozy, then a scrubby. People called it weird ... and we went with it! Now we try and think up weirder and weirder things to knit! If you have any ideas, please contact us at our contact info below!

We love knitting, and we hope that you love the things we knit! That's why we made this website, to show you a little bit about what we're all about! Enjoy 😊
